# Glossary

## Loaf

A cat sitting with all four paws tucked under its body, resembling a loaf of
bread. The site is built around this concept.

## Mochi

The calico cat featured on the page. Used as the cat's name in the subtitle
and below the photo.

## Hero

The main centered section containing the pill, headline, subtitle, and
framed photo. The "above the fold" content.

## Pill

The small rounded outlined chip near the top of the hero showing
"♥ CALICO CAT".

## Photo frame

The bordered, shadowed, rounded container that wraps the cat image and gives
it a soft "physical" feel.

## Botanicals

The accent-colored line-art branch illustrations placed to the left and right
of the photo for atmosphere. Hidden on mobile.

## CTA

Call-to-action button. The page has two: "View Full Photo" (filled) and
"About This Cat" (outlined). Both currently link to `#`.
